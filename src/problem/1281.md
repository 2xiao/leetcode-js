**Example 1:**

> Input: n = 234

> Output: 15
>
> Explanation:

> Product of digits = 2 _ 3 _ 4 = 24
>
> Sum of digits = 2 + 3 + 4 = 9

> Input: n = 4421
>
> Output: 21
>
> Explanation: Product of digits = 4 _ 4 _ 2 \* 1 = 32
>
> Sum of digits = 4 + 4 + 2 + 1 = 11
>
> Result = 32 - 11 = 21

**Constraints:**

- `1 <= n <= 10^5`
