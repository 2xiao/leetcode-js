**n-queens puzzle**_.

![](https://assets.leetcode.com/uploads/2020/11/13/queens.jpg)

> Input: n = 4
>
> Output: 2
>
> Explanation: There are two distinct solutions to the 4-queens puzzle as shown.

**Example 2:**

> Input: n = 1
>
> Output: 1

**Constraints:**

- `1 <= n <= 9`
