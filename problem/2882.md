> ```

>

> | customer_id | name    | email               |

> | 2           | David   | michael@example.com |

> | 4           | Alice   | john@example.com    |

> +-------------+---------+---------------------+
> ```
>
> Explanation:
>
> Alic (customer_id = 4) and Finn (customer_id = 5) both use john@example.com, so only the first occurrence of this email is retained.
